C5.8 Strong and Weak Acids
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Lemon - Citric acid
Milk - Lactic acid
Coke - Carbonic acid
Green tea - Tannic acid
Orange - Ascorbic acid
Vinegar - Ehtanoic acid

A dilute solution is one in which there is a relatively small amount of solute dissolved in the solution

A concentrated solution contains a relatively large amount of solute

Strong acid comp. ionises with aq solution
Weak acid part. ionises with aq solution
