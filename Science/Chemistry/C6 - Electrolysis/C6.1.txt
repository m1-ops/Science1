C6.1 Intro to Electrolysis
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Cation (POSITIVELY CAHRGED IONS) ---------> Cathode (NEGATIVELY CHARGED ELECTRODE)
Anion (NEGATIVELY CHARGED IONS) ---------> Anode (POSITIVELY CHARGED ELECTRODE)

2NaCl --> 2Na+ + 2Cl-

An electrolyte is an ionic substance that has been melted or dissolved in water so that the ions are free to move and conduct electricity
