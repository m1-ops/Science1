C6.2 Changes at the electrodes
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Cathode:
        Pb2+ + 2e- --> Pb
Anode:
        2Br- --> Br2 + 2e-

Ex.
    CuBr2 --> Cu2+ + 2Br-

    Cu2+ + 2e- --> Cu (reduced)
    Br- --> Br2 + 2e- (oxidised)

in aqueous solutions (halide ions > hydroxide > all other negative ions) (anode)
in aqueous solutions more reactive than hydrogen (hydrogen forms)
